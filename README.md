# CRM (Customer Relationship Management) Module for FrontAccounting

Advanced customer relationship management with sales automation and customer analytics.

## Features

- **Customer Profiling**: Comprehensive customer profiles with history
- **Sales Pipeline**: Sales opportunity and pipeline management
- **Lead Management**: Lead tracking and conversion management
- **Customer Segmentation**: Automated customer segmentation and targeting
- **Communication Tracking**: Track all customer interactions and communications
- **Campaign Management**: Marketing campaign planning and execution
- **Customer Analytics**: Customer behavior and value analysis

## Requirements

- FrontAccounting 2.4+
- PHP 8.0+
